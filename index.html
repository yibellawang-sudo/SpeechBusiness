FAQ

The stuff people
always ask

If something's still unclear, just call us — that's what the free consultation is for.

Where are sessions held?

Sessions are held online via Zoom, so you can join from anywhere. All you need is a stable internet connection and somewhere quiet to sit.

Is there an in-person option?

Right now we run everything online only. It keeps scheduling flexible and means we can work with students no matter where they're based.

What ages do you coach?

We primarily coach middle and high school students, but our consulting programs are open to anyone — professionals included. Not sure if you fit? Just ask us.

How long is a session?

Standard sessions are one hour. Courses run 8 sessions across roughly one month. If you bundle a full course, your coach is also available to review your work between sessions at no extra charge.

What happens after I click "Book a session"?

You'll fill out a short form and we'll reach out within 24 hours to confirm your details, match you with the right coach, and lock in a time that works for you.

Can I try one session before committing to a course?

Yes — the Targeted Series drop-in rate ($25/hr) lets you try a session before committing to the full course. The Fundamentals of Communication course is a full-program commitment only.
